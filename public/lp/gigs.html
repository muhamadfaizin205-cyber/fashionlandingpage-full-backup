Why Order Design Directly Instead of Through a Marketplace

On Fiverr or Upwork you are sifting through hundreds of sellers of wildly varying quality, comparing portfolios, and gambling on who actually delivers. Communication runs through platform messaging with response delays, and source files are often withheld unless you paid for the right tier.

Dean Designers is a single studio with one designer. You know exactly who is doing the work. You communicate directly via WhatsApp. Every package includes source files and full commercial rights with no exceptions.

What Makes These Files Production-Ready

The gap between a design that looks good on screen and one that survives manufacturing is where most brands lose money. Colors shift on fabric. Fine lines vanish in screen printing. Gradients band in DTG. Transparent areas behave unpredictably.

Every file we deliver is prepared for your specific printing method - proper color separations for screen printing, optimized profiles for direct-to-garment, full-bleed layouts for sublimation, and simplified paths for embroidery. Your manufacturer accepts the file without asking questions.
