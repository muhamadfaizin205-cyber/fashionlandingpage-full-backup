Custom Hoodie Design Service

The hoodie is the flagship garment of streetwear. It carries the largest print area, commands the highest price point, and does the most work in defining how a brand is perceived.

Designing for the Hoodie Canvas

A hoodie offers design real estate that a t-shirt cannot match. The back panel is a billboard. The chest supports a logo or focal graphic. Sleeves accommodate vertical typography or repeating elements. The hood itself can carry text. Front pockets create natural framing.

Using this space well requires thinking in terms of a complete garment, not a single graphic dropped onto fabric. The elements need to relate to each other, guide the eye, and hold together whether the wearer is standing still or in motion.

Dean Designers approaches hoodie design as a system: how the front reads from across a room, how the back functions as the statement piece, and how the details reward closer attention.

Print Techniques and Their Constraints

Different print methods produce dramatically different results on heavyweight fleece. Screen printing delivers bold, opaque color and holds up to washing but has practical color limits. Puff print adds dimensional texture that photographs beautifully. Embroidery brings premium tactile quality but demands simplified artwork. DTG handles complex color and gradients but sits differently on thick fabric.

We design with your chosen technique in mind from the start, and deliver files prepared specifically for it. If you are unsure which method suits your budget and aesthetic, we will advise before design begins.
